How it Works

1. Choose your goal & register

Choose your goal for the Level Up challenge and then register online. You can choose 600 cardio/140 strength, 1200 cardio/400 strength, or 2400 cardio/600 strength minutes as your goal. Once you choose your goal, if you want to LEVEL UP in the challenge, your goal can be changed in your profile. Once you register, you will be sent a confirmation email with your YTRAAK confirmation number and login instructions.

REGISTER NOW

Cardio Goals are based on the American Heart Association's recommendation of at least 150 minutes of cardio excercise per week.

Strength Goals are based on the American College of Sports Medicine's recommendation of 20-60 minutes per day, 2-3 days per week.
